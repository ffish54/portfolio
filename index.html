more about me

I first gained a passion for computer science during my childhood, as I lived with only my mother, who was deaf, sometimes it was hard to verbally communicate with her, so I started to use technology like typing what I wanted to say or just messaging her, because of this, I quickly gained an affinity to computer and devices as they helped me speak to my parent. After this and throughout my life, my interest and experience with technology and digital media have grown.


In school, I took computer science as a subject and enjoyed learning about programming and software development. I continued this by studying computer science at A-level, where I further developed my skills in programming languages such as Python and JavaScript. I am excited to continue my journey as a software developer and look forward to the opportunities and challenges that lie ahead.
